Project Overview

This project analyses employee attrition patterns using the IBM HR Analytics dataset and presents business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard.

The objective was to identify key factors contributing to employee turnover and provide recommendations to improve employee retention.

Business Questions
Which departments experience the highest attrition?
Which job roles are most at risk?
Does overtime influence employee turnover?
Is employee income associated with attrition?

Key Findings
Sales and Human Resources recorded the highest attrition rates.
Employees working overtime showed significantly higher turnover.
Lower income groups demonstrated greater attrition risk.
Sales Representatives represented the highest-risk employee segment.

Business Recommendations
Review overtime policies and workload allocation.
Strengthen employee retention programmes in Sales.
Benchmark compensation structures against market standards.
Introduce targeted engagement initiatives.
